The OSG is here to support your research computing needs. We offer an array of services for researchers to take advantage of including access to the OSPool which has provided 244 Million Wall Hours to researchers this year.

Power my research with OSG Contribute to OSG
The OSG is a consortium of research collaborations, campuses, national laboratories and software providers dedicated to the advancement of all open science via the practice of distributed High Throughput Computing (dHTC), and the advancement of its state of the art.

Submit Locally, Run Globally

  • International scope of OSG makes it likely an OSG member is in your region.
  • National Caches and Pools made to improve your network experience.
  • More members means better performance, contact us if you are interested in contributing.
